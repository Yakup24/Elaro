# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Elaro to be a respectful and welcoming project. Everyone participating in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions is expected to act with professionalism and care.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Stay focused on the technical work.
- Accept responsibility and correct mistakes quickly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Any behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

Security issues should be reported through `SECURITY.md`, not through public issue threads.
